Hi, my name is Dan. I am a serial entrepreneur and product manager living in London.

After close to 20 years in startups, I am now working as Head of Product, Digital Screening at NHS England. Together, we are building a new technology platform for the 10 million people who are screened each year and the NHS staff who deliver these services.

You can read more about me, browse my archive, check out my photos, subscribe to my blog using RSS or connect via LinkedIn. I also like to rate (and occasionally review) films on Letterboxd.
