Sixt Car Boot Comparison

I don't own a car and so when my family and I need a car we rent. More often than not we'll rent from Sixt: they are local to us, the prices are reasonable and their customer service is good. Like all car rental companies, Sixt make it hard to understand how big a car's boot is. Is two small bags and one medium one enough? What does two large bags mean? And so on. The size of every car boot is freely available, so I can only assume the uncertainty generates more revenue.

To make my life easier I made this Sixt car boot comparison table. Some things to note:

  • This is not every Sixt car, just the ones I have rented.
  • Each boot size is measured with the seats up.
  • In my experience, you need a boot as big as the one in a Volvo XC60 (483 L) to go camping with a family of four.

I hope you find it useful!
